## 1. What useAnchor Is

useAnchor is a personal safety companion application. It allows users to create temporary "Anchor Sessions" before meetups or travel, notify trusted contacts, and trigger alerts if they feel unsafe or fail to check in.

**useAnchor is not an emergency service.** It does not contact police, ambulance, or fire services on your behalf. It notifies the trusted contacts you personally select. In a genuine emergency, always contact local emergency services directly in addition to using useAnchor.

## 2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to create an account. If you are between 13 and 18, you may only use useAnchor with the involvement and consent of a parent or guardian, where permitted by local law.

- You are responsible for the accuracy of the information you provide, including your phone number and trusted contact details.
- You are responsible for keeping your account secure.
- You may delete your account at any time; see the Privacy Policy for what happens to your data when you do.

## 4. Trusted Contacts

- You are responsible for obtaining consent from anyone you add as a trusted contact before or immediately after adding them.
- Trusted contacts may decline or remove themselves from your network at any time.

## 5. Acceptable Use

You agree not to:

- Use useAnchor to harass, stalk, or monitor another person without their knowledge or consent.
- Provide false emergency information or trigger false alerts.
- Use the trusted contact or session features to track someone against their will.
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to useAnchor's systems.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate this section.

## 6. No Guarantee of Delivery or Response

useAnchor makes reasonable efforts to deliver alerts quickly and reliably, but we cannot guarantee:

- That push notifications or SMS messages will be delivered instantly or at all (this depends on third-party networks, device settings, and connectivity).
- That a trusted contact will see, read, or act on an alert.
- That GPS location data will be accurate, especially indoors or in areas with poor signal.

**You should never rely on useAnchor as your only safety measure.** Use it alongside — not instead of — sound personal judgment and, where appropriate, contact with emergency services.

## 7. Location Data

By creating an Anchor Session, you consent to useAnchor collecting your device's location for the duration of that session, for the purpose of sharing it with your trusted contacts if an alert is triggered. See the Privacy Policy for full detail on how location data is stored, used, and deleted.

useAnchor is a tool to support personal safety decisions; it does not assume responsibility for user safety outcomes.
